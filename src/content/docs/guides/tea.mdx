### Tips ###

:::danger[Limit Cut]
If the blasts hit direct E/W for the first set that means group 1 goes **WEST**.
:::

:::note[Time Stop]
The blue tether is yolo'd in PF, make sure you check if you are on the Cruise side or not.
:::

:::caution[Light Beacon]
Standing on the A marker for Final Word as Light Beacon will most likely kill someone, please stand a little North of the marker to prevent wipes.
:::

:::tip[Fate Cal Beta]
If you are one of the Dark Debuff players make sure to go towards the wall to bait your jump before doing the stack/spread!
:::
